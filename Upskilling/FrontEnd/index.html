City Council News Bulletin

Annual Summer Festival Announcements

Welcome to the official updates section of our local city portal. This weekend, the municipal council is kicking off our annual community engagement program, featuring interactive town halls, music workshops, and outdoor heritage walks designed for residents of all age brackets.

Please note that several streets surrounding the main square will be temporarily zoned off for pedestrian-only accessibility. We highly encourage utilizing public transit lines or accessing the geolocation mapping utility built into this portal to locate alternative parking structures smoothly.

Volunteer sign-ups remain open until Thursday afternoon. If you are interested in contributing to event operations, logistics management, or helping run our digital information booths, please fill out your contact metrics inside our online portal registry above.
